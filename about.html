About Redline Authority

Built from experience. Focused on results.

Redline Authority was created with one goal — to provide platform-focused remote tuning backed by real-world data and over 15 years of LS and LT calibration experience.

After tuning more than 2,000 vehicles ranging from stock optimized vehicles to full blown 1000+ HP monsters, the philosophy remains the same: Safe reputable power with consistent results in a reasonable time frame.

This isn’t a “tune everything” operation. Redline Authority is dedicated specifically to the LS and LT platforms using HP Tuners and Holley EFI.

Every calibration is built around log analysis — not canned files. Clear communication, structured revisions, and mechanical honesty are part of the process.
