Redline Authority - Simple Website

Files:
- index.html (Home)
- contact.html (Contact)
- intake.html (Build + Payment)
- styles.css
- assets/logo.png (your generated logo)

Quick start:
- Open index.html in a browser, or deploy to Vercel/Netlify.
- Replace youremail@domain.com in contact.html and intake.html.
- Replace the payment links (#) in intake.html with your Stripe/PayPal links.

Recommended upgrades:
- Swap mailto forms to Formspree for clean inbox submissions.
